This is a maven project ran on a Tomcat v8.0 server, this project was to make it easier to keep track of all your cryptocurrency. With so many different currencies and different wallets its hard to keep track of how much currency you have and where it is. This application allows you to log your wallet addresses and where they come from. It is also able to take the address and find the amount in USD that wallet contains and gives you a total of your crypto investments.
